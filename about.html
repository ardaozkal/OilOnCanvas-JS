About OOC

OOC stands for Oil On Canvas. It's a wastefully raw and sarcastic image format.
It was designed as a meme by some friends, and converter was developed by Arda Ozkal.
The file type is simple: Every rgba value and pixel is splitted by a comma. Height is determined by the amount of lines.
To give an example, this is how a 3x3 plus sign looks in OOC: 255,255,255,255,0,0,0,255,255,255,255,255\n0,0,0,255,0,0,0,255,0,0,0,255\n255,255,255,255,0,0,0,255,255,255,255,255\n
